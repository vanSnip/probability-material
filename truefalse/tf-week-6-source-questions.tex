\begin{truefalse}
Eve's law says that $V(X) = \E{\V{X|N}} + \V{\E{X|N}}$.

Claim: $\E{\V{X|N}}$ is called the \emph{in-between} group variation.

\begin{solution}
False.
\end{solution}
\end{truefalse}
